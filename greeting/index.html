A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and passed away on July 27, 2015. He was known as the "People's President" for his friendly and accessible nature.

Dr. Kalam was a renowned scientist who played a key role in the development of India's civilian space program and military missile systems. He was also known for his contributions to India's nuclear weapons program.
